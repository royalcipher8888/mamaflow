1
Cybersecurity
Definition: The practice of protecting systems, networks, devices, and data from unauthorized access, misuse, disruption, or harm.
Protecting accounts, devices, and information
Preventing unauthorized access
Reducing risk from digital threats
Responding to and recovering from incidents
Say it plain
"Cybersecurity is about protecting systems, people, and data from unauthorized access, misuse, and harm."
Said this definition out loud ✓
2
Digital Hygiene
Definition: The ongoing habits and practices individuals use to maintain security, privacy, and safety in digital spaces.
Using strong, unique passwords
Keeping software and devices updated
Recognizing suspicious messages
Enabling MFA on all accounts
Say it plain
"Digital hygiene is how people lower their risk over time — just like brushing teeth lowers the risk of cavities."
Said this definition out loud ✓
3
Privacy vs Security
Privacy: The ability to control how personal data is collected, used, shared, and stored.
Security: The set of protections and safeguards that prevent unauthorized access to systems and data.
Privacy = Control · Security = Protection · You need both.
Say it plain
"Privacy is about control. Security is about protection. They work together — but they are not the same."
Said both definitions out loud ✓
4
MFA
Definition: Multi-factor authentication is a security method that requires two or more forms of verification to access an account.
Something you know — password
Something you have — phone, authenticator app
Something you are — biometrics
Say it plain
"MFA adds a second lock to the door — even if the first lock fails, your account stays protected."
Said this definition out loud ✓
5
Risk
Definition: The potential for loss or harm when a threat exploits a vulnerability, resulting in an impact.
Risk = Exposure + Vulnerability + Impact
Exposure — what is accessible or visible
Vulnerability — a weakness that can be exploited
Impact — the damage that would occur if exploited
Say it plain
"Risk isn't about fear — it's about understanding what could go wrong and how bad it would be if it did."
Said this definition out loud ✓
